Scenario:

LOCATION:

Baltimore, MD

Population: 622,793

Area: 92.98 mi²

HOUSEHOLD:

Family of 4. Both adults work.

One child is 4, the other is 8.

MEDIAN INCOME:

$69,402

$4,577/month

$45,552 above federal poverty level based on the median per capita income.

Ideal Budget:

Monthly pay ($4,577) divided into what you should be spending in each category.

Needs Total:

Adding each item in the needs category to find out what is being spend on average on necessities.

Actual Budget:

Taking the total amount spent on needs then figuring out if needs are under budget and what is left for wants and savings.

Needs are $1,759 and 38% over the ideal budget which leaves this family little room for items they may want like a car or a cell phone or having any money to put towards savigns or debt repayment.
