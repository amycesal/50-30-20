The 50/30/20 Rule

People will be in solid financial shape if they use:

50% of their income for things they need, mandatory expenses like:

  • housing
  • utilities
  • health insurance
  • groceries
  • childcare

30% for things they want, expenses like:

  • cable & internet
  • babysitting and after-school activities
  • sporting events
  • dinners out
  • hobbies

20% on building their savings and paying down debt:

  • credit cards
  • student loans
  • savings and retirement accounts
